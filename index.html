Why Mixed Pops?

The flavor you need, the creaminess you deserve — Mixed Pops!

Each Mixed Pop starts with fresh, only-once-used dairy and high-quality real fruit pulp frozen at precisely −16°C. Our proprietary slow-churn process locks in that signature creamy texture while keeping every pop crunchy on the outside.

From the kitchen of Svada Tastes & Flavours, we believe every bite should be an adventure. With flavours spanning tropical fruits, nuts, and indulgent confections, there's a pop for every palate.

What makes Mixed Pops different from regular ice cream?

Mixed Pops are slow-churned at 20% overrun and frozen to precisely −16°C, giving them a uniquely dense, gelato-like creaminess inside a satisfying crunchy outer coating. We only use fresh dairy and real fruit pulp — never artificial flavours.

Where can I buy Mixed Pops?

You can order directly via our website (www.mixedpops.com), visit our café & shop, or catch our food truck. We also offer delivery through major food delivery platforms.

Do you offer catering for events?

Absolutely! We cater for weddings, corporate events, birthday parties, and more. Contact us at +91 012345689 to discuss your requirements and get a custom package.

Are your products suitable for vegetarians?

Yes! All Mixed Pops are 100% vegetarian. We use high-quality dairy and real fruit pulp with no animal-derived gelatin or rennet in any of our products.

What flavours are currently available?

We offer 12 flavours including Mango, Strawberry, Blackberry, Sitaphal, Coconut, Avocado, Jackfruit, Tender Coconut, Pistachio, Almond, Truffle, and our premium Lotus Biscoff. Seasonal specials are added throughout the year!
